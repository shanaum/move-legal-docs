Overview

MOVE ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • Media Content: Photos and videos you create, edit, or upload within the app
  • User Preferences: App settings, selected templates, and customization choices
  • AI Interactions: Prompts and requests you submit to our AI features

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
  • Performance Data: App crashes, errors, and performance metrics

3. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To capture photos and videos for content creation
  • Photo Library: To access, edit, and save your media content
  • Microphone: To record audio for video content
  • Motion & Orientation: To optimize video stabilization and camera controls

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  1. Provide Core Functionality
    • Generate AI-enhanced content and social media posts
    • Apply templates and effects to your media
    • Save and export your created content
  2. Improve Our Services
    • Analyze app usage to enhance features
    • Fix bugs and optimize performance
    • Develop new AI models and templates
  3. Communicate With You
    • Send important updates about the app
    • Respond to support requests
    • Provide feature announcements (with your consent)

Purpose: Generate AI-enhanced content based on your prompts

Data Shared: Your text prompts and selected media (processed securely)

Backend Services

Purpose: Provide secure authentication and data management

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your created content is primarily stored on your device
  • Cloud Processing: Media may be temporarily uploaded to our servers for AI processing
  • Encryption: All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Data Retention:
    • AI processing data is deleted immediately after processing
    • Cached trending data is refreshed every 2 hours
    • We do not store your personal media on our servers

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  1. Access Your Data: Request a copy of data we have about you
  2. Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your information
  3. Opt-Out: Disable analytics or optional features
  4. Export Content: Export all your created content from the app

To exercise these rights, contact us at ildar901122@gmail.com

MOVE is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your media content with others without your permission
  • Use your content for training AI models without consent

We MAY share data:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
